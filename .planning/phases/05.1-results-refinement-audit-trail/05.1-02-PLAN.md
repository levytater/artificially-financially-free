**2. Create `src/components/results/audit-breakdown-table.tsx`:**

Create the AuditBreakdownTable component with these specifications:

**Props:** `{ results: ComparisonResult }`

**Data extraction:** Build audit rows from existing `ComparisonResult` data — no recalculation. For each year in `results.yearlyComparison`:
- Buyer side: Get corresponding entry from `results.housingProjection.yearlyProjection[i]` for homeValue, remainingBalance, homeEquity, mortgagePayment, propertyTax, maintenance, totalAnnualCost
- Renter side: Get annualRent, monthlySavings from `results.yearlyComparison[i]` and portfolioBalance from `results.portfolio[i].endBalance`
- Net worth difference: `renterNetWorth - buyerNetWorthWithSelling`

**Layout:** Use the 3-focused-table approach from research to avoid column overload:

**Section 1: "Home Equity Growth"** (5 columns)
| Year | Home Value | Mortgage Balance | Home Equity | Annual Appreciation |

Note: Annual appreciation = homeValue[year] - homeValue[year-1] (or homeValue[0] - purchasePrice for year 1)

**Section 2: "Cost Comparison"** (5 columns)
| Year | Buyer Annual Cost | Annual Rent | Monthly Savings | Cumulative Costs |

**Section 3: "Net Worth Progression"** (4 columns)
| Year | Renter Portfolio | Buyer Equity (net of selling) | Difference |

Use `<h3>` headings for each section. Each section in its own `<div className="overflow-x-auto">` for independent scrolling.

**Styling:**
- Table: `min-w-full text-sm`
- Header: `bg-muted/50 text-xs uppercase tracking-wide`
- Rows: `border-t hover:bg-muted/30 transition-colors`
- Use alternating background on even rows for readability: `even:bg-muted/10`
- Net worth difference column: color-code positive (green/text-green-400) vs negative (red/text-red-400) values
- All currency values: use `formatCurrencyDecimal` with compact=false for precision
- Wrap each table in `<div className="print-keep-together">` for print support (CSS class defined in Plan 03)
